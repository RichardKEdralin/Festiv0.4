What is Festiv?

Festiv is your one stop for discovering and discussing festivals and other events. Very often people try to plan early for music festivals and concerts but the company website does not always have all the information you need. Festiv provides a community for people to discuss the many different festivals that you want to attend as well as allow you to discover new festivals and events.
